Hawksley Centrifuge Design

First project I took after University. The job was to analyse the market of centrifuges in the small labs sector, generate a brief identifying key design opportunities and execute a product. The final result was a fully working prototype, along with a costed bill of materials. I worked closely with manufacturers and suppliers. I took the project a step further, I identified all the medical regulation required to pass the centrifuge. Conducted the conformity tests in house and wrote the necessary paperwork.
