Automated Mural Painter

For my Final Year Project, I was set the task of creating a new product that improves/automates the production of a task. Then, generate a business plan and Kickstarter video. We spotted an opportunity in the mural sector as the average price for a mural was out of range for many individuals. By automating this process whilst retaining artistic ambiguity in the system, we could capture a new market.
