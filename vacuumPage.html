Designing a Rumba-like-Vacuum

The task, to analyse an existing vacuum purchased from Amazon. Then select a brand, and incorporate the brands style into an improved version of the vacuum. By improved, I mean easier and cheaper to manufacture and if possible with better functionality.

The process can be seen as this:

  1. Design Analysis
  2. Brand Selection
  3. Early Concept Design
  4. Design Development
  5. Final Design: Comparison, drawing & render
